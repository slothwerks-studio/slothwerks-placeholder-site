About SlothWerks

Located in Grand Rapids, Michigan, SlothWerks provides web and mobile application development and user experience consultation services.

We harness twenty years of technical experience to craft solutions for both commerical and non-profit clients throughout West Michigan.

Contact SlothWerks by phone at 616.258.6179 or by email at contact@slothwerks.com. You may also connect with us on Slack.

About Grand Circus

Grand Circus is a tech career training organization based in Detroit, Michigan. They also maintain a campus at Start Garden in Grand Rapids, Michigan.

Intro to Code Workshops

SlothWerks provides instruction for Grand Circus' monthly Intro to Coding workshops. Find a workshop near you on the Grand Circus events calendar.

About the Eastgate Neighborhood Association

The ENA serves the Plymouth Heights area in Grand Rapids and offers special event programming, community outreach, and neighborhood resources to ENA residents.

Friends of Mulick Park

SlothWerks provides technical support for the Friends of Mulick Park, which serves as a community liaison to Mulick Park Elementary. Learn more about Mulick Park on the Grand Rapids Public Schools website.
